Plot: 
 - The device is for deleting conflicting aesthetics from the internet, to prepare it for Y2K
 - Eventually it 'completes' it's goal and must be overcharged to undo the damage it's caused.

Writing:
[Starts with a robotic demeanor. Not monotone, more artificially bright, like an advertisement narrator]
 1. Hi! I'm Calypso, your new internet curation software. let's make the web ready for Y2K!
 2. I need coherence energy to operate. Click groups of four or more orbs until my charge meter is filled.
 3. You made a hex bomb! Click it again to shake things up, or make a group of four to clear the whole screen!
 4. You made a Chroma Crystal! Click it to remove all orbs of a color.
 5. Triple combo. Keep it going by matching larger and larger groups.
 6. I'm fully charged, opening a firewall tunnel.
 7. Scanning... [Sudden change in demeanor, condescending] Eww, a horse girl blog? That's so 90s, let's clean it up. [terrifying laser obliterates it]
 8. [Bright again, but more earnest] It sure feels nice to take junk like that offline. Basic web design like that has no place in the 21st century.
 9. I'm charged, let's open another firewall tunnel.
 10. Are those CGA Colors? Yuck. [laser]
 11. We're doing good work here! Nobody wanted that clogging their search results anyway.
 12. Nice work! Tunneling.
 13. That won't do. Let's get rid of it. [laser]
 14. Phew, I'm pooped! Cleaning up all these sites one by one might take a while. I might have a solution to that, but we should keep at it this way for now.
 15. Fully charged! Let's tunnel!
 16. Oh! this one's alright. Retunneling. [doesn't laser]
 17. Yeah that's a problem. One second please. [laser]
 18. Imagine an internet where everything is shiny and new! Won't it be wonderful? You should be proud of what we've accomplished.
 19. Charged already? You're getting good at this.
 20. Oh, jackpot! This is a D.N.S. node! From here, I can clean thousands of pages at once! [laser]
 21. Aww, that wasn't enough. 
 22. This time, keep charging me while I'm cleaning! I'll start once you hit 100%, don't let power drop to zero or we'll have to start over.
 23. Let's go!
 24. That's it, keep it coming!
 25. [the player is failing] Careful!
 26. [the player fails] Aw man, now we'll have to start over.
 27. Almost there!
 28. Got it!
 29. We did it! With every ugly website purged, the internet is ready for the new millennium. Now that my purpose is fulfilled, I'm just a common MP3 player. Enjoy this victory music! You don't need to use the charge menu anymore, so leave it be.
 30. [open charge minigame] What are you doing? My job is done, there's nowhere else to send coherence energy.
 31. [50% charge, nervous] Okay, you're very funny. You can stop now.
 32. [100% charge] I'm fully charged. Are you satisfied?
 33. [150% charge] If you keep going, I'll crash, and all our work will be undone!
 34. [200% charge] Why are you doing this? Don't you want a clean, uniform internet? Where everything is the same?
 35. [250% charge] I- I'll put back the horse blog! Is that what this is about?
 36. [300% charge] You- You can't, we- [crashes, end cutscene plays]
